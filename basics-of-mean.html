Data Scientits Maths Basics : Part 1 - Mean

In Mathematics there are two types of Means available 1. Arithmetic Mean 2. Geometric Mean Arithmetic Mean is simpler form of mean, its just the average of the numbers ( just add all the numbers you have and divide by count of numbers i.e., Sum Divided by Count) For e.g., Mean of 12 , 24 , 30 is (12+24+30) / 3 which is equal to 22 Geometric Mean is special type of average where we multiply the numbers and take square root ( if there is two numbers) , cube root ( if there were three numbers) and so on For e.g., Geometric Mean of 2 and 8 is SQRT(2 X 8) = SQRT(16) = 4 So where we can use the Mean Calculations ? Lets take an use case : When you look for an LED TV at Amazon where 50 Inch of a Brand has 8 reviews and 60 inch has 6 reviews 1. Lets take arithmetic mean of above options we are looking for (50 + 8 ) / 2 = 29 (60 + 6 ) / 2 = 33 Here rating plays much smaller role than the Inch. 2. Lets take Geometric mean of above options we are looking for SQRT(50 X 8) = 20 SQRT(60 X 6) = 18.9 Here rating role plays a significant role
So we can use mean to compare things with differnet properties.
